## ☕ CoffeeBoi

**CoffeeBoi** is an adorable desktop companion who just wants one thing:
for you to drink the coffee you *already made* before it goes cold. 🥺

He's a soft-spoken femboy in a cozy cat-themed outfit, and he shows up every so often to gently remind you that your beverage is waiting. Whether he's worried, bashful, or just excited to see you, Coffee Boi is always there with a warm heart and a sweet little nudge. Just a cozy digital guy looking out for you. 💖

This app uses Windows notifications with a randomized mix of titles and reminders. It's entirely configurable within the config.json file to change the frequency of the reminders, among other settings.

### Features:

* Transparent, always-on-top sprite of your personal coffee boi.
* Rotating, charming messages full of charm and only *mild* emotional blackmail.
* System tray icon support with snooze/quit options.
